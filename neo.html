Luxmundis Cup

The Luxmundis Cup at Tangub City Global College is a prominent event that embodies athletic camaraderie, sportsmanship, and competition. It serves as a platform for students to showcase their physical skills in various intramural sports and activities. The event is designed to promote community spirit, unity, and holistic student development, aligning with the college's mission to foster well-rounded individuals. The name "Luxmundis," which translates to "Light of the World," reflects the institution's commitment to being a guiding light in education and personal growth​
